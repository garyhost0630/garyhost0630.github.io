Hey! I am a Ph.D. student at Boston Univeristy advised by [Prof. Tianyu Wang](https://tyw-lab.github.io). I started my graduate studies in 2024 after graduating with a B.S. in Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering from Shenzhen University, working with Prof. Xiao Peng and Prof. Junle Qu. 

I am broadly interested in optical imaging, particularly multiphoton imaging techniques for studying biological systems. My research focuses on developing and applying advanced multiphoton imaging methods to neuroscience, with the goal of enabling more precise observation and analysis of brain activity.

Feel free to reach out if you are interested in my work or would like to collaborate.
Let’s do great work and have fun along the way!
